Less a guide for trading, more a tour of the associated worldview. I've encountered much of this already over the years by way of economics, interviews with people with a finance background, and "general online exposure". This is the most concise summary of this kind of reasoning and the associated mental models I've seen so far. Recommended reading, even if you keep your money in a cheap broad index fund, as you probably should and which reading this will make you _more_ likely to do.
