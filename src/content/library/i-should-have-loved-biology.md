I did not "get" biology in school either — I did well enough, but it felt like memorization and I never really cared about it. I discovered it for real in my 20s, after falling down a bit of a rabbit hole via a hacker type's youtube video on a [DIY genetic engineering project](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J3FcbFqSoQY), and this incredible essay really captured my feelings about the subject once things started clicking into place and I grokked its significance.
